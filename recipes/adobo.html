Milkfish Adobo

A dish that brings you home to the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup light soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 3 bay leaves
  • pepper
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup of water
  • milkfish cleaned and sliced crosswise into serving pieces

Steps

  1. Airfry milkfish.
  2. Saute onion first then garlic.
  3. Combine light soy sauce, dark soy sauce and vinegar in a bowl. Add to the pan along with water and bay leaves.
  4. Add the milkfish and cook till the sauce boils. Afterwards, let it simmer for 10 minutes.
