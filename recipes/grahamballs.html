Graham Balls

Home

A yummy dessert that is fun to make and can be enjoyed by all ages.

Ingredients

  • graham crackers
  • condensed milk
  • cocoa powder
  • marshmallow

Steps

  1. Crush graham crackers into fine crumbs manually or by using a food processor.
  2. Add condensed milk and mix. Add some more condensed milk if the texture is dry.
  3. Grab about 1 tbsp of the mixture then flatten to add a marshmallow. Roll into a circle.
  4. Coat the ball in cocoa powder for an additional darker taste.
